About the Business

Richmond Artisan Bakery & Café has been a cornerstone of the Richmond community for over two decades. Founded in 2002 by a passionate baker who wanted to bring European-style artisan bread to Melbourne's inner suburbs, the business has grown from a small wholesale operation into a beloved neighbourhood institution with a thriving café component.

The business operates six days a week, with the kitchen starting at 4am to ensure fresh baked goods are ready for the morning rush. The café seats 32 covers indoors and a further 18 on the footpath. It is well known locally for its sourdough loaves, croissants and seasonal specials, and has been featured in several Melbourne food publications over the years.

Revenue is split approximately 60% café and 40% wholesale, with wholesale supply agreements in place with three local restaurants and a corporate catering client. The business has a strong and loyal regular customer base — many of whom have been coming in daily for over a decade.

Why It's For Sale

The owner, who founded the bakery in 2002, is retiring at 67 after a career dedicated to the craft of baking. He has no desire to wind the business down — quite the opposite. He is immensely proud of what he's built and wants to see it continue under someone who shares his values around quality, community and craftsmanship.

This is not a distressed sale. The business is profitable, fully staffed and operating well. The owner is simply ready to hand over the keys and enjoy retirement. He is willing to stay on for a handover period of up to three months to ensure a smooth transition.

Other Information

Trading hours: Tuesday to Sunday, 7am–3pm. Closed Mondays. Kitchen operates from 4am.

Online presence: 4.8 stars on Google (312 reviews). Active Instagram following of 4,200. Regular coverage in local community newsletters and Richmond food guides.

Equipment: The sale includes two commercial deck ovens, a full commercial kitchen fit-out, espresso machines, a refrigerated display cabinet, POS system and all smallwares. A full asset register is available on enquiry.

Growth opportunities: The current owner has deliberately kept the business manageable. A new owner with ambition could expand wholesale supply, introduce online ordering, extend trading hours or add catering services — all of which have been requested by existing customers.

Parking: Street parking available directly outside. Nearby council car park two minutes walk.
