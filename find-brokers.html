Why Use a Broker

Sometimes you need someone in your corner

Selling a business you've spent years building is one of the most significant financial decisions of your life. A good broker brings experience, buyer networks, negotiation skills and a process that protects your interests from start to finish.

The brokers listed here specialise in retirement sales — they understand the emotional weight of the decision, not just the financial mechanics.

Or list your business directly — it's free to start →
🎯

Access to qualified buyers

Brokers maintain active buyer networks and can match your business to the right person faster than a public listing alone.

💼

Professional valuation

A good broker helps you understand what your business is worth and positions it correctly for the market.

🤝

Negotiation support

Having an experienced negotiator between you and the buyer takes the emotion out of the process and protects your outcome.

📋

End-to-end process management

From information memorandum to due diligence to settlement — a broker manages the process so you can focus on the business.
