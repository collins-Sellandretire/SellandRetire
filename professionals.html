Who We Work With

Four types of professionals retiring owners need

Get listed in our referral network and be introduced to sellers and buyers at exactly the moment they need your expertise.

Business Brokers

Help sellers find buyers and close the deal

Our sellers are motivated, often emotional, and dealing with complex transactions. They need brokers who genuinely understand the retirement sale context — not just someone who lists businesses on a database.

  • Referred directly to sellers who are ready to engage a broker
  • Listed profile on our platform visible to all visitors
  • Featured in our seller onboarding resources
  • Monthly introductions to new seller enquiries in your state

Business Lawyers

Guide clients through contracts and due diligence

Business sales involve complex legal documentation — heads of agreement, contracts of sale, restraint clauses, employee entitlements. Our sellers and buyers need specialists, not general practitioners.

  • Referred at the point sellers enter due diligence
  • Featured as a trusted legal partner on our platform
  • Access to both seller and buyer referrals
  • Listed in our resource guides and FAQs

Accountants

Navigate the tax implications of selling

Capital gains, GST on business assets, super strategies, small business concessions — selling a business has real tax complexity. Our sellers need accountants who specialise in this, not just annual returns.

  • Referred during the listing preparation stage
  • Introduced to sellers needing valuation and tax advice
  • Featured in our seller preparation resources
  • Ongoing referral relationship as your reputation builds

Business Valuers

Give sellers and buyers a credible independent figure

A price mismatch between seller and buyer is the single biggest blocker to a retirement sale completing. An independent valuation resolves this early and keeps transactions moving.

  • Referred at the earliest stage of seller onboarding
  • Listed as an approved valuer on our platform
  • Buyer due diligence referrals as well as seller referrals
  • Featured in our How It Works seller resources
