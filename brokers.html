Why Sell & Retire

A platform built for the listings you specialise in

General business-for-sale platforms mix retirement sales with distressed sales, franchises and startups. Your listings get lost. Here, every buyer came specifically looking for what you're selling.

🎯

Targeted buyer audience

Every buyer on Sell & Retire registered specifically to find a business from a retiring owner. No tyre-kickers, no bargain hunters — buyers who understand and respect what retirement sales involve.

⚡

List in minutes

No lengthy onboarding. Send us your listing details and we'll have it live the same day. Photos, financials, key features — we format it professionally and get it in front of buyers fast.

📣

Your brand on every listing

Every listing you place clearly shows your brokerage name. Buyers who enquire know who you are. It's additional brand exposure with every listing and every enquiry.

📊

Complement your existing platforms

We're not asking you to move away from Seek Business or Bsale. Add Sell & Retire as another channel and increase your listing's reach to a highly targeted retirement-focused audience.

🔒

Confidentiality protected

Buyer details are collected before sharing with you. NDA templates available. Your vendor's identity and financials stay private until you decide to share them with serious enquirers.

💰

Simple, honest pricing

No success fees. No lock-in contracts. Pay per listing or choose a monthly plan if you have multiple listings. You keep 100% of your commission — we just help you find the buyer faster.
