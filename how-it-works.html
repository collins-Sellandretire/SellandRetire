The Process

Your journey from owner to retiree

We've designed every step to be straightforward, confidential, and respectful of the life's work you're entrusting us with.

📋
Step 1

Create Your Listing

Tell us about your business — the history, the numbers, what makes it special, and what you're looking for in a buyer. Our listing process is designed to bring out the best in your story, not reduce it to a spreadsheet.

Very quick, easy and straightforward to complete

Every listing is reviewed before it goes live

Step 2

Buyers Discover You

Your listing goes live to buyers who are specifically searching for retirement handovers. Unlike generic platforms, every buyer here knows they're dealing with a retiring owner — and that matters to the right kind of buyer.

Registered buyers are matched to new listings as they go live
Your contact details stay private until you're ready
Anyone can enquire — no registration required

Step 3

Receive Enquiries

Interested buyers submit an enquiry directly through the platform — no registration required, no barriers. You receive their name, contact details and message. You decide who's worth your time.

Enquiries are forwarded to you directly
Each buyer provides their background and intent
You choose who to engage with and when

Step 4

Connect & Negotiate

Meet serious buyers, discuss terms, and take the conversation to the next level. This is where having the right support matters most — we can connect you with accountants, business lawyers and brokers who specialise in retirement sales.

We can refer specialist accountants, lawyers and brokers if you need them
🎉
Step 5

Complete the Sale & Retire

Contracts signed, keys handed over, and your next chapter begins. What you do from here is entirely yours.

Quick Answers

Common questions

How long does it take to sell?

It varies. Some businesses sell within weeks; others take 6–12 months. Having a realistic price, good documentation and a clear story speeds things up significantly.

Do I need to use a broker?

No. Many sellers sell directly through our platform. But for complex or high-value businesses, a broker can be worth it — we can refer you to specialists.

What if I don't know what my business is worth?

We can refer you to a qualified valuer. Most businesses are valued at 1–3× annual profit, but it varies significantly by industry.

Can I keep my listing confidential?

Yes. Your business name, exact location and financial details can all be kept private until you're ready to share them with specific buyers.
