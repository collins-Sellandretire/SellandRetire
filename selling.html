Preparation Tips

What makes a great listing

Businesses with complete, honest listings sell faster and attract better buyers. Here's what to have ready.

📊 Financial Documents

  • Last 3 years of profit & loss statements
  • Most recent BAS lodgements
  • Current balance sheet
  • List of assets included in the sale
  • Any outstanding liabilities

🏢 Business Information

  • Lease agreement details (if applicable)
  • Staff numbers and employment arrangements
  • Key supplier and customer relationships
  • Licences, permits and registrations
  • Any IP, systems or processes you own

📸 Photos & Story

  • Good quality photos of the premises
  • Photos of equipment and fit-out
  • Your business story — how it started, what makes it special
  • Your reason for selling
  • What a handover period might look like
