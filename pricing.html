Quick Answers

Pricing questions answered

Is it really free to start?

Yes. You can list your business and receive buyer enquiries at no cost. Founding members — the first 50 sellers — get 12 months completely free.

Do you charge a commission on the sale?

No. We charge listing fees only — not a percentage of your sale. There's no conflict of interest in our model.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can start on the free period and upgrade to Starter, Standard or Premium at any time. We'll apply any remaining time proportionally.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit and debit cards via Stripe. GST invoices are issued automatically. No lock-in contracts.
